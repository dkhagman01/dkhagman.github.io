    Cloudiness vs. Latitude:
    The data generated reaffirm prior observations indicating no strong relationship between latitude and cloudiness. Curiously though there appears to be a hint of 80-100% cloudiness immediately around the equator, that reappears at higher latitudes. There are also clear bands of cities at 0, 78, and 100% cloudiness, with weaker ones also appearing at 20 and 40%.
